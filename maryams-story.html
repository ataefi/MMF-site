Maryam’s Story

How did she become Maryam?

Maryam’s story is one of faith, courage, sacrifice, education, immigration, family, service, and love. Her life was not easy — but she remained gentle, giving, and full of light.

“A love like hers does not leave people empty.

It continues carrying them… even after a soul has gone home to God.”

A life of becoming

Maryam carried beauty through hardship.

She lived through upheaval, sacrifice, migration, work, study, motherhood, service, illness, and loss — yet she never allowed suffering to make her bitter.

Instead, she became more tender. More prayerful. More generous. More devoted to the dignity of every soul who crossed her path.

Timeline

The chapters of a luminous life.

Iran

Roots, faith, and early gifts

Maryam’s early life formed the foundation of her character: devotion to family, love of beauty, reverence for education, and a deep spiritual sensitivity that shaped everything she later became.

Fashion & Creation

A natural artist and teacher

Maryam had an extraordinary gift for fashion, sewing, design, and creation. She did not simply make beautiful things — she taught, encouraged, and helped others see what was possible.

Work & Study

Intelligence, discipline, and perseverance

Throughout her life, Maryam kept learning. She studied, worked, adapted, and grew, even when circumstances demanded more from her than most people could bear.

Revolution & Departure

Courage through uncertainty

Maryam endured revolution, religious persecution, and the pain of leaving behind a homeland and people she loved. She carried that loss quietly, but never allowed it to harden her heart.

Pakistan

A chapter of transition and sacrifice

During a difficult and uncertain period, Maryam continued to protect, serve, and love her family with the same devotion that defined her entire life.

America

Building a new life

Maryam came to the United States seeking freedom, opportunity, and a better future for her children. She worked tirelessly, carried her family, and gave more than anyone fully understood.

Education

Learning as dignity

Maryam believed in education not as status, but as transformation. Her commitment to learning continues through the Foundation’s scholarship, mentorship, and educational programs.

Service

A home where people felt safe

Maryam fed people, prayed for people, welcomed people, comforted people, and noticed needs before they were spoken. Her home was not just a place. It was a refuge.

Legacy

Love that continues

The Maryam Mottaghi Foundation exists so that her love continues moving outward — through education, service, mentorship, creativity, compassion, and hope.

Education

She believed people could rise.

Maryam’s life teaches that education is not only about degrees. It is about dignity, courage, access, confidence, and the chance to become more fully oneself.

That belief lives at the heart of the Foundation.

Family

She loved without keeping score.

Maryam’s love was not conditional. It did not wait until someone deserved it. It was steady, generous, forgiving, and full.

She loved with the kind of tenderness that becomes a person’s compass long after they are gone.

Her light

She is not gone from the work.

Every act of service, every scholarship, every mentorship, every meal, every story, every moment of care offered through this Foundation is a continuation of her light.
