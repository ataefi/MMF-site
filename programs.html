Programs

Transforming remembrance into opportunity.

The Foundation’s programs are designed to carry Maryam’s values into action — through education, mentorship, creativity, practical support, and compassionate service.

Built from the way Maryam loved.

Maryam did not help people in only one way. She taught. She cooked. She sewed. She prayed. She encouraged. She protected. She noticed need before it was spoken. The Foundation’s programs reflect that fullness.

Each initiative is meant to help someone move forward with more confidence, dignity, skill, belonging, and hope.

Opening doors

Scholarships

Scholarship support helps learners continue forward when cost, circumstance, or hardship threatens to interrupt their education.

This program honors Maryam’s belief that learning is not merely achievement. It is dignity, confidence, independence, and possibility.

Guidance

Mentorship

Mentorship provides encouragement, practical support, and steady guidance for students and young people navigating educational, personal, or professional barriers.

Maryam had a way of helping people feel capable before they fully believed it themselves. This program carries that spirit forward.

Creation

Fashion Design

Fashion design programming honors Maryam’s extraordinary gifts in sewing, design, creativity, and teaching.

Through hands-on learning, students can explore beauty, craft, patience, precision, self-expression, and the confidence that comes from making something with their own hands.

Access

Technology

Technology programming helps learners build digital confidence, practical skills, and access to modern educational and career pathways.

The goal is not technology for its own sake. It is access, independence, problem-solving, and the ability to participate fully in a changing world.

Hospitality

Culinary Arts

Maryam’s cooking was never only food. It was welcome. It was abundance. It was care made visible.

Culinary programming honors that spirit by teaching skill, nourishment, cultural memory, hospitality, and the quiet dignity of feeding others with love.

Voice

Writing & Publishing

Writing support helps learners and storytellers develop voice, memory, reflection, personal narrative, and pathways to publication.

This program exists because stories matter. Words can preserve truth, honor experience, heal memory, and help people understand themselves and one another.

Truth

Journalism & Media

Journalism and media programming supports responsible storytelling rooted in research, clarity, ethics, curiosity, and integrity.

Students learn that telling a story is a responsibility: to listen carefully, verify thoughtfully, and communicate with humanity.

Story to screen

Filmmaking

Filmmaking programming teaches the art and discipline of visual storytelling — from idea and interview to image, sound, editing, and audience.

The goal is to help young storytellers understand not only how to use tools, but how to honor the people and stories in front of them.

Care in action

Community Support

Community support provides practical, compassionate help guided by dignity, discretion, and love.

This work reflects one of Maryam’s clearest qualities: when someone needed help, she did not look away. She moved toward them.
